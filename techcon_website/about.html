Our History

TechCon began over a decade ago as a small meetup for developers passionate about innovation. Over the years, it has evolved into a global conference, attracting industry leaders and startups alike. The event has consistently showcased groundbreaking technologies and set the stage for future tech revolutions.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to connect brilliant minds in technology, inspire innovation, and create opportunities for collaboration that shape the future. We strive to promote inclusivity, sustainability, and advancement in every aspect of technology and community development.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

A visionary mathematician and the first computer programmer, Dr. Lovelace’s pioneering insights continue to inspire generations of technologists at TechCon.

Alan Turing

A foundational figure in computer science, Turing’s contributions to artificial intelligence and computational theory remain central to the TechCon spirit.

Grace Hopper

A programming pioneer and Navy Rear Admiral, Grace Hopper’s advocacy for practical computing and innovation has been celebrated in multiple TechCon sessions.
