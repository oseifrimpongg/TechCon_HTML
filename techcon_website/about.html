History

TechCon started as a regional meetup in 2013 and has grown into an international conference that highlights emerging technology, hands-on workshops, and cross-industry collaboration. Each year the event expands its reach and diversity of topics.

Mission

Our mission is to connect builders, leaders, and learners to accelerate innovation responsibly. TechCon focuses on inclusivity, practical solutions, and sustainable growth across tech communities.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Bloom

Dr. Bloom is a leader in machine learning research whose work on explainable AI has informed ethical guidelines used across industry.

Alex Rivera

Alex is a founder and CTO who scaled multiple startups; their talks on resilient systems and culture have been widely cited by engineering teams.

Prof. Grace Lin

Prof. Lin’s research on human-computer interaction shaped several accessibility best practices adopted at TechCon.
